Removed the `heuristic` speaker namer and the `auto` namer policy. Regex name-inference could not be made accurate enough to be safe (a wrong name is worse than none), so naming is now opt-in and the default is `off` (speakers keep their `SPEAKER_xx` labels). A persisted `EARWIG_NAMER=heuristic`/`auto` now warns and falls back to `off`.
